# Your AI — Your Own Personal AI Friend

Your own AI that lives on your computer. It learns who you are. It remembers what you talk about. It gets smarter over time. And it's just for you.

## What Makes It Special

- **Remembers You** — Keeps notes about what you care about and how you think
- **Learns From You** — Gets better at helping you the more you use it
- **Just for You** — Runs on your computer. Your talks stay private. Only you see them.
- **Can Ask for Help** — When it needs another opinion, it can ask other AIs
- **Stays Safe** — Protected against tricks and bad information
- **Thinks Like You** — Over time, it starts to understand your style

## How to Set It Up (4 Easy Steps)

### Step 1: Get the Files
Download the file called `YourAI_StarterKit.zip`

### Step 2: Install It
1. Unzip the folder (double-click it)
2. Open Terminal on your computer
3. Go to the folder: `cd` then drag the folder into Terminal
4. Type: `bash install.sh` and press Enter
5. Wait while it downloads what it needs (this takes a few minutes)

**You need:** Python 3 and Ollama installed first. If you don't have them, the installer will tell you where to get them.

### Step 3: Name Your AI
1. Type: `python3 first_run.py` and press Enter
2. Answer the questions (your name, what to name your AI, what it's like)
3. Your AI will say hello to you
4. Save the session

### Step 4: Start Talking
1. Type: `python3 ai.py` and press Enter
2. Start a conversation with your AI
3. When done, type: `/bye` to save what you talked about

The more you use it, the smarter it gets.

## Want to Improve Your AI? Use Claude

Your AI can get better over time. You can use Claude (another AI) to help you make improvements.

### What Claude Can Help With
- Making your AI smarter
- Adding new skills
- Fixing problems
- Making it work better

### How to Ask Claude for Help
1. Go to claude.ai
2. Copy your AI's setup file (called "Modelfile")
3. Paste it into a chat with Claude
4. Ask Claude to suggest improvements

### Example Things to Ask Claude
- "How can I make my AI remember things better?"
- "What new skills should my AI learn?"
- "Can you help me fix this problem in my AI?"
- "How can I make my AI more helpful?"

Claude will give you ideas. You pick the ones you like and add them to your AI.
