# Your AI — A Private Intelligence That Belongs to You

A beautiful, locally-run AI that learns who you are and develops specifically for you. Not a product. A relationship.

## What's Inside

Your own private AI with:
- **Soul Architecture** — Three-layer identity grounded in Aristotle's philosophy
- **Persistent Memory** — Every saved session builds your relationship with your AI
- **Learning Loop** — Extracts lessons and detects its own capability gaps
- **Brain Functions** — Default Mode Network, predictive priming, Theory of Mind
- **Immune System** — Three-line biological defense against manipulation
- **Roundtable** — Consults Gemini, Groq & Claude when it needs more perspectives

## Get Started

1. **Download** — Get `YourAI_StarterKit.zip` from the link below
2. **Install** — Run `bash install.sh` (requires Python 3 and Ollama)
3. **Personalize** — Run `python3 first_run.py` to meet your AI and name it
4. **Use** — Start sessions with `python3 ai.py`

The more you use it, the more it knows you.
